# Memory Dream Game

A contemplative narrative game about a factory worker who dies in his sleep and wanders through a white world, reliving memories from different periods of his life.

## Story

You wake up to find yourself in a vast white space. Your memories surround you—moments from your childhood, your first day at work, and the peaceful final sunset. Walk through this ethereal world and discover the story of a life well-lived.

## Game Features

- **2D Top-Down Exploration**: Walk freely in a minimalist white world
- **Memory Discovery System**: Encounter different memories as interactive zones
- **Atmospheric Storytelling**: Relive key moments from various life stages
- **Minimalist Art Style**: Simple geometric shapes with soft colors
- **Contemplative Gameplay**: No combat, just reflection and discovery

## Controls

- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD**: Move the character
- **Mouse Click**: Interact with memory zones (coming soon)

## Installation

1. Install [Godot Engine 4.2+](https://godotengine.org/)
2. Clone this repository
3. Open the project in Godot
4. Press Play or F5 to run
